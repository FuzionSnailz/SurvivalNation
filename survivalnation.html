Welcome to SurvivalNation, Entertainment at it's Finest

SurvivalNation in short, is an Entertainment & Tech Company. To expand on this, we create and specialize in a couple of different areas. Some of these areas include video games, board games, TCG, movies, animation, comics, music, software, hardware, and networking. We plan to expand as much as we can to incorperate even more, and make the world a better, more positive place. This is a place where discipline, ideas, imagination, and dreams thrive.

Why SurvivalNation

Have you ever had an idea for a game you wanted to play? Have you ever had an idea for a comic book character you wantes to see? In today's world, you can't really just go out and ask a studio or company to make that for you. You can rarely even get companies to take customer feedback and improve on what their customers actually want. Well, here at SurvivalNation, we want our community to be very involved and give them the opportunities to express their ideas while we assist in bringing them to life. We also want to give opportunities to those who like. This could be by voice acting, coding, designing, modeling, becoming an artist, and much more.
